Text Styles

Title Styles

Here are some styles for titles and headings.

H1: Title or Page Heading

  • Usage: main page title only
  • font-size: 3rem or 48px
  • font-weight: bold or 700
  • y-axis margin: 1.5rem or 24px

H2: Subtitle

  • Usage: article titles or section headings or sub-titles
  • font-size: 2.5rem or 40px
  • font-weight: bold or 700
  • y-axis margin: 1rem or 16px

H3: Heading

  • Usage: subtitles in articles or section sub-headings
  • font-size: 1.75rem or 28px
  • font-weight: semi-bold or 500
  • y-axis margin: 1rem or 16px
